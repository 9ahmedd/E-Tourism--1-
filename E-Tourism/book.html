Package Details

Located in a quiet area in Sharm El Sheikh, Verginia Sharm Resort & Aqua Park features a large swimming pool with a waterfall and fountains, and a spa. The rooms have a balcony, while the suites offer views of the pool area and palm trees. Each unit is air-conditioned and features a satellite TV, minibar and free Wi-Fi. Among the facilities at the hotel is a fitness center for guests who wish to work out. Afterwards, guests can relax in the health club, which includes a spa bath, hammam and sauna. Verginia Sharm Resort & Aqua Park offers a diving center where guests can explore the amazing underwater fauna and landscapes of the Red Sea. Children can play safely in the children's pool. The professional entertainment team takes care of children's entertainment throughout the day, and at night they can enjoy live entertainment shows in the hotel's amphitheater. The property has 2 restaurants serving a variety of cuisines, including seafood, international food and oriental specialties. Ghadamas Bar offers cocktails and live music. Verginia is 65 km from the coral reef sites of Ras Mohammed National Park. Sharm El Sheikh International Airport is just a 15-minute drive away, and free private parking is available on site. The property currently has 2 swimming pools, a family one with a water park and the other a quiet pool for adults only (16 years or older).
