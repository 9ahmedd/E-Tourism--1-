Package Details

Overlooking the ancient Luxor Temple, Nefertiti offers comfortable rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom. Luxor Railway Station is only a 10-minute walk from the hotel. Nefertiti's rooms enjoy a simple interior design. Some rooms have a private balcony. Nefertiti has a tour desk that provides cruises on the Nile River and many tourist trips in Luxor. Convenient 24-hour front desk service is also available. A variety of drinks and Egyptian dishes are served on the rooftop terrace. Here, guests can also smoke a traditional Egyptian hookah. It is a 20-minute drive from Luxor International Airport
