Package Details

Jaz Resorts Almaza Bay is located in Almaza Bay. It features a white sandy beach, 9 swimming pools, 6 children's pools, 4 Ã  la carte restaurants and 15 bars. All rooms at Jaz Almaza Bay have a balcony or terrace overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. Each room has a flat-screen TV, minibar, wardrobe and telephone. Some rooms include a spacious seating area and interconnecting rooms. Guests can enjoy a wide variety of international dining venues to choose from, including 3 fine dining restaurants. Morgana serves Mediterranean cuisine outside, Corallo offers dining options from a menu featuring seafood while enjoying views of the Mediterranean Sea, and Sofra features authentic Lebanese cuisine. Jaz Almaza Bay features a spa that includes a range of relaxation options such as: Sauna, steam room and hot tub. There is also a fully equipped gym and a beauty salon including treatment rooms at a cost. The property offers a beach lodge where guests can enjoy quiet time and food and beverage services provided by a private butler. Guests are required to book and apply additional costs. A range of sports activities are available on site such as table tennis, billiards, beach volleyball, water polo and a water gym. Other activities available include parasailing, kayaks and paddle boards at a cost. Children can also enjoy daily activities at the kids club.
